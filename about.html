About New Laxmi Bakers

New Laxmi Bakery, also known as Laxmi Bakes and Cakes, is a well-regarded bakery in Gadhinglaj, Maharashtra, known for its variety of fresh and flavorful baked goods. 🍰

The bakery has been serving the community for over 40 years and is especially popular for its 100% eggless cakes. 🥐🎂

We started in 1980 in Gadhinglaj, Maharashtra. We’re proud to offer traditional eggless cakes, sweets, and bakery items. 🥰
